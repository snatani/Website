Welcome!

I'm a Process Engineer at Applied Materials in the Dielectric Deposition Products team. I completed my PhD at UC San Diego advised by Prof. Prabhakar Bandaru and Prof. Andrew Kummel.

My thesis focused on interfacial engineering for next gen logic and memory devices based on 2D/1D materials. A list of the projects I worked on can be found here. Majority of the research I did during my PhD was sponsored by TSMC Research and focused on ultrathin ALD gate oxides for 2D materials and threshold voltage control through gate stack engineering and surface treatments for sub-nm EOT top gated FETs on 2D TMD materials.
Please reach out to me if you are interested in knowing more about my research or if you are interested in collaborating on any of the projects listed in the Research section.
