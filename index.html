Welcome!

A PhD candidate at UC San Diego advised by Prof. Prabhakar Bandaru and Prof. Andrew Kummel.

My research interests lie in fabrication and modeling of nanoscale devices based on 2D/1D materials. I am currently working on a project sponsored by TSMC Research on ultrathin ALD gate oxides and threshold voltage control through gate stack engineering and surface treatments for top gated FETs on 2D TMD materials.
Please reach out to me if you are interested in knowing more about my research or if you are interested in collaborating on any of the projects listed in the Research section.
