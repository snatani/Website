Welcome!

A PhD candidate at UC San Diego advised by Prof. Prabhakar Bandaru and Prof. Andrew Kummel.

My research interests lie in fabrication and modeling of nanoscale devices based on 2D/1D materials.

Education

In the past, I have twice interned at Adobe w/ Kalyan Sunkavalli, Vladimir Kim and at NVIDIA Research w/ Stan Birchfield . I have also worked as a staff researcher at UC San Diego School of Medicine with Fransisco Contijoch and was a visiting researcher in the Bio Robotics Lab with YU Haoyong at National University of Singapore. I received my M.S. degree in Computer Science from UC San Diego and B.Eng. degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from BITS Pilani, where I was advised by Surekha Bhanot. My research is supported by the Qualcomm Innovation Fellowship 2023.
